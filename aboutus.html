Who we are?


OzTap is a new tech start-up founded by Industry professionals committed to innovation. The focus is to drive non-intrusive and seamless technology solutions to enhance the overall customer experience.

Though Hospitality and technology enablement are in our DNA, David and Julian have over 30 years of experience delivering excellence in customer service across multiple industries, Our passion is to bring OzTap to a broad cross section of industries from Retail, to Travel & Tourism with a mission to provide unique solutions to new and existing problems, while always keeping the customer at the forefront of everything we do.
